My goal is to advance generative models to better capture and represent the real world. I have contributed to this by enhancing the controllability of image and video diffusion models. I aim to expand their impact by improving temporal quality and achieving more realistic motion representation, integrating diverse modalities such as text prompts and 3D attributes.
